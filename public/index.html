    Life is busy and getting busier. Sometimes technology is the problem causing all kinds of distractions but it can also be your friend. mindBot is a chatbot that uses introspective questioning and AI to help you organize your mind and concentrate on doing what matters most to you. Our mission is to help you understand your thought patterns and how you can make sure you are not led astray by them but use them rather to lead to positive behaviors to live an optimised life, helping you manage your intentions and be accountable for the goals you have set yourself.
